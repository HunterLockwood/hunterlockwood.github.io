About

Professionalia

I work in the Language Research Office at The Myaamia Center under Dr. David Costa. The Myaamia Center is the Miami Tribe of Oklahoma's research center on Miami University's campus in Oxford, Ohio.

I finished my Ph.D. in Linguistics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison in August 2017. Under the supervision of Dr. Monica Macaulay, my dissertation, How the Potawatomi Language Lives: A Grammar of Potawatomi translates the name the elders gave The Forest County Potawatomi Dictionary, which I helped compile and edit while I was in graduate school.
