"New School" Tabletop Gaming: SWORDDREAM

During the initial waves of covid-19, I discovered (alongside many others) a love of tabletop roleplaying games. Initially drawn to relatively complex & complete simulations like Pathfinder 2e , my preferences have shifted away from corporate-owned games, to indepenent games with a more artpunk aestheticand do-it-yourself attitude. In this tradition of play, the focus of play tends to be more on on interaction, imagination, and creative problem solving. Less emphasis tends to be places on memorizing complex rules (who has time for that??) and meaningless violence.
