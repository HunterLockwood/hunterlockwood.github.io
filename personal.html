"New School" Tabletop Gaming: SWORDDREAM

During the initial waves of covid-19, I discovered (alongside many others) a love of tabletop roleplaying games. Initially drawn to relatively complex & complete simulations like Pathfinder 2e , my preferences have shifted away from corporate-owned games, to independent games with a more artpunk aesthetic and do-it-yourself attitude. In this tradition of play, the focus of play tends to be more on on interaction, imagination, and creative problem solving. Less emphasis tends to be places on memorizing arcane rules (after all, who has time for that??) and meaningless violence.
