🍄Rhizomes & Replantings🪴

In what's plotted here, the following inspirations can be observed:

  • The Zettelkasten & Digital Gardening approaches
  • Groups & movements that reflect my own, phantasmagorical memories of an Internet that used to exist, for example:
    • geminispace and other alternative web protocols
    • ...but see this blogpost by Daniel Stenberg, creator of curl, for an excellent critique of geminispace
    • indieweb & small-web
    • comparable movements in gaming, like the SWORD DREAM.
  • A number of tools designed to process markdown, including:
    • OneNote, which I stopped using a number of years ago but deserves honorable mention for years of service,
    • Obsidian, of course; I actually had a paid subscription for it, but I ended up wanting something sleeker and more minimal,
    • LogSeq, an excellent FOSS competitor to Obsidian
